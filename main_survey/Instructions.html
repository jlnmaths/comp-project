Dear participant,

Welcome to our experiment!
Please read the following instructions carefully. All statements in the instructions are true. Your payoff depends on how well you have understood the instructions.
Participants remain anonymous throughout the whole experiment and the collected data will be analyzed anonymously.

In the following, you will be asked to make investment decisions. In each decision, you allocate your portfolio between a safe and a risky asset. The safe asset has a fixed return of 3 %. The risky asset has a return that is distributed according to a normal distribution with mean of 6 % and a standard deviation of 17.8 % (matches the average return of the S%P500 over the past 100 years). After you have made this decision once, you will receive financial advice, then you will be asked to make choose again.

We will randomly draw one of your two decision and pay you according to the returns your portfolio has accrued, using a draw from the distribution specified above to determine the return of the risky asset. Specifically, if your portfolio has a return of x %, you will receive $ x.
